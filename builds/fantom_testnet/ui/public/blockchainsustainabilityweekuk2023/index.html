About The Event

About The Event

The JobCrypt Blockchain Sustainability Week - UK 2023 is a 5 day celebration of global efforts in the blockchain industry to tackle issues such as climate change and inequality. The event will see presentations from experts in both Sustainability and blockchain as well as provide you with actionable ways to get involved with the movement and make a difference.
