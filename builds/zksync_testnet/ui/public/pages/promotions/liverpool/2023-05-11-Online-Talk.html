JobCrypt Liverpool Talk Series - Event


Introduction to DAOs in Sustainability

This event will introduce attendees to the basics of Decentralized Autonomous Organisations (DAOs) and how these new types of organisation are using blockchain governance to make strides in the battle for a Sustainabile future. It will be your opportunity ask questions and gain answers on the foundations of the new Green Revolution with some insights on how you can get involved. We will also be working in small with the Filecoin blockchain and sending decentralized letters to tomorrow which have some exciting features. If you're early you might get some free crypto. If not fear not we have more SWAG to give away through the course of the evening, so do join us.


Date

When? May 11th 2023
What time? 17:45 - 20:00 GMT +1 (UK Time)
Location? Online

17:45 - 17:59 Ice Breakers Swag giveaway and icebreaker competitions

In this talk we will examine some of the DAOs thare are working to address Sustainability issues around the world.

In this session we will help you send a letter to tomorrow using the Optimism blockchain

In this talk we take a look at how to join and contribute to a DAO
